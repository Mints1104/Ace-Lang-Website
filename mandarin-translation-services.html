Supporting Chinese Business & Community in Wales

Wales has strengthened its economic ties with China significantly in recent years, with numerous Welsh companies establishing operations in China and Chinese businesses investing in Wales. Additionally, Wales hosts a growing Chinese student population and business community, particularly in Cardiff and Newport.

Our Chinese translation team includes native speakers from mainland China, Taiwan, and Hong Kong with expertise in both Simplified and Traditional Chinese. We understand the cultural nuances essential for successful Welsh-Chinese business relationships and provide accurate, culturally appropriate translations that respect Chinese business etiquette and communication styles.
